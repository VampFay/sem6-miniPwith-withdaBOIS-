# Minimum Controlled Record Fields

Every instantiated form receives a unique record ID, controlled template revision, product/release/
site scope, author/event/entry dates, attachments with hashes, reviewers, decisions, signatures and
change history. The eQMS implementation may separate these forms but may not remove required fields.

## Design or change review

Reason and scope; affected claims/requirements/hazards/code/model/data/dependencies/infrastructure/
labeling/sites; clinical, regulatory, privacy, security and usability impacts; verification and
revalidation required; reviewers and independence; issues/actions; decision; baseline identifiers.

## Nonconformance, complaint or incident

Source and awareness time; affected patient/specimen/site/version; description and evidence;
potential harm; containment; classification; reportability per market with deadline/rationale;
investigation/root cause; corrections/CAPA; communications; effectiveness; closure approvals.

## CAPA

Problem and scope; data/trend; immediate correction; root-cause method/evidence; systemic extent;
action plan/owners/dates; risk and regulatory impact; validation; effectiveness metric/sample/time;
result; recurrence check; closure or escalation.

## Training and competence

Person/role; document/task/revision; prerequisite; method; trainer; completion; assessment and
acceptance; authorization scope; expiry/reassessment; signature.

## Supplier evaluation

Service and criticality; requirements; qualification evidence; QMS/security/privacy/license status;
agreement/change notification; continuity/exit; risks/controls; monitoring criteria; decision and
re-evaluation date.

## Release record

Exact source/container/SBOM/model/configuration/labeling hashes; authorization and site scope;
gate/evidence manifest; anomalies and residual risk; build/verification/backup/rollback evidence;
approvers; release time; distribution; revocation criteria.
