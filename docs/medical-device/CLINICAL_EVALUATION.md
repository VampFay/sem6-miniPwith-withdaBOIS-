# Clinical Evaluation Plan — Framework

## Prerequisites

Approve intended use, reference standard, eligible specimens, supported organs/tumors, scanner and
staining envelope, endpoints, clinically acceptable errors, subgroup definitions, and regulatory
path. Resolve PanNuke's non-commercial license; research benchmark performance cannot establish
clinical validity.

## Study sequence

1. Analytical verification on locked, traceable datasets: repeatability/reproducibility, robustness
   to color/focus/compression/tissue artifacts, resolution bounds, edge/empty cases, and failure
   detection.
2. Retrospective external multi-site validation with patient/site-level independence and a locked
   statistical analysis plan. Compare against expert consensus/adjudication and predefine handling
   of ambiguous nuclei and missing data.
3. Prospective silent-mode validation in real laboratory workflow to measure distribution shift,
   integration failures, turnaround, exclusions, and override/rejection patterns without affecting
   care.
4. Human-factors/workflow validation and, if claimed, a reader or clinical-utility study comparing
   standard care with assisted care.

## Statistical controls

Predefine primary/secondary endpoints, multiplicity, sample-size/power, confidence interval method,
noninferiority/superiority margin if applicable, missingness, cluster effects, site heterogeneity,
reader variability, subgroup minimums, acceptance thresholds, and locked analysis code. Report all
exclusions and failures. No pivotal-set tuning, selective tissue reporting, or post-hoc threshold
changes. An independent biostatistician and qualified pathologists approve the protocol and report.

## Required evidence

Protocol/registration, ethics and data approvals, data provenance and representativeness report,
annotation/reference-standard SOP, blinded analysis log, deviations, full statistical report,
failure gallery, subgroup analysis, clinical safety assessment, and signed clinical evaluation
report. None of these artifacts currently exists in this repository.
